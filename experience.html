My Experience

Background Actor (2024)

I had the chance to be a background actor in Game of Life, directed by Syafiq Yusof. It was a meaningful experience that gave me a glimpse into the real environment and excitement of the acting world.

Banquet (2023-2024)

I gained part-time work experience as a banquet staff at Mardhiyyah Hotel in Selangor. It was a great opportunity to develop my communication and teamwork skills.

Freelance Photographer (2024)

While waiting for my UPU results, I took up photography jobs to fill my time. Most of the projects I worked on were related to sports and family events, which helped me sharpen my skills and gain valuable experience.
